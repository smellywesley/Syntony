# HandVoice Evidence Appendix v1

This appendix supports the canonical competition architecture. It does not control implementation scope.

## Smartphone motor and speech precedent

### Li et al., 2024 — TapTalk device validation

**Title:** Smartphone automated motor and speech analysis: Validation of TapTalk across 20 different devices  
**Use:** Establishes smartphone tapping, DDK speech, combined tapping/counting, MediaPipe-based movement analysis and cross-device technical validation.  
**Source:** https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11496774/

### Alty et al., 2024 — TapTalk protocol

**Title:** Development of a smartphone screening test for preclinical Alzheimer’s disease and related disorders: the TapTalk protocol  
**Use:** Supports short smartphone-administered tapping, DDK and dual cognitive–motor tasks.  
**Source:** https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s12883-024-03609-z

## Speech measurement

### Rusz et al., 2018

**Title:** Smartphone Allows Capture of Speech Abnormalities Associated With High Risk of Developing Parkinson's Disease  
**Use:** Supports smartphone-derived pause timing, speech timing and F0 variability.  
**Source:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/29994713/

### Sousa et al., 2025

**Title:** Digital speech biomarkers can measure acute effects of levodopa in Parkinson’s disease  
**Use:** Supports sustained phonation, `/pa-ta-ka/`, reading, DDK timing, pauses, prosody and repeated measurement.  
**Source:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41531-025-01045-5

### Cao et al., 2025

**Title:** Speech and language biomarkers for Parkinson’s disease prediction, early diagnosis and progression  
**Use:** Supports task-aware speech architecture rather than one generic voice model.  
**Source:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41531-025-00913-4

## Finger-tapping measurement

### Islam et al., 2023

**Title:** Using AI to measure Parkinson’s disease severity at home  
**Use:** Supports interpretable home-video tapping features and explicit handling of noisy recordings.  
**Source:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41746-023-00905-9

### Yang et al., 2024

**Title:** FastEval Parkinsonism  
**Use:** Supports MediaPipe keypoints, recording confidence and multi-angle robustness.  
**Source:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41746-024-01022-x

### Ehsan et al., 2026

**Title:** Interpretable and granular video-based quantification of motor characteristics from the finger-tapping test in Parkinson’s disease  
**Use:** Supports granular hypokinesia, bradykinesia, sequence-effect and hesitation features.  
**Source:** https://www.nature.com/articles/s41531-026-01307-w

## Dual-task rationale

### Ho et al., 2002

**Title:** The effect of a concurrent task on Parkinsonian speech  
**Use:** Supports the attentional sensitivity of speech production.  
**Source:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11935422/

### Whitfield et al., 2019

**Title:** Effects of concurrent manual task performance on connected speech in Parkinson disease  
**Use:** Supports analyzing speech deterioration during simultaneous manual activity.  
**Source:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31306612/

## Evidence boundary

The papers support the component measurements. They do not establish that HandVoice’s event-coupling metric is clinically meaningful, disease-specific or longitudinally reliable. Those remain research questions.
