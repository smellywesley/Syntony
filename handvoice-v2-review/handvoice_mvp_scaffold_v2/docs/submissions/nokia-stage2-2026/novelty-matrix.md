# Novelty and alternatives matrix

| Alternative | What it already does | HandVoice distinction | Honest limitation |
|---|---|---|---|
| TapTalk | Smartphone tapping, DDK and device validation | Bidirectional motor–speech cost and synchronized event/QC timeline are central outputs | HandVoice has no comparable human/device validation yet |
| Separate smartphone tapping test | Quantifies manual rhythm | Adds simultaneous articulatory loading and same-clock comparison | Added information is still a research hypothesis |
| Separate speech/DDK test | Quantifies speech timing | Adds concurrent manual loading and motor effect | Energy onset detector is unvalidated |
| Clinician observation | Context-rich human assessment | Reproducible numerical trace and capture-quality reasons | Cannot replace clinical judgement |
| Do nothing / informal follow-up | Zero new burden | Proposed short, structured measurement | Need and adoption remain to be confirmed |

Frozen differentiation:

> A staff-assisted smartphone protocol that measures manual and articulatory
> rhythm separately and together, then shows transparent within-person
> fixed-order within-session contrasts on one synchronized, quality-checked
> timeline; current speech-onset outputs are exploratory and unvalidated.
