# Problem and workflow validation gate

Do not collect recordings, diagnoses, health histories or identifiable clinical
information. These are stakeholder-discovery conversations, subject to the
university's guidance.

## Required sample

- At least 3 rehabilitation, community-care or allied-health professionals.
- At least 2 older adults, caregivers or credible workflow proxies.

## Decision rule

Continue with a community-care workflow claim only when at least two of three
professionals independently confirm the same recurring need and name a concrete
human action supported by the output. Otherwise, remove the care-workflow claim
and reposition HandVoice as research instrumentation or stop the concept.

**Current decision:** OPEN — no interviews are recorded in this repository.
